Resources

Free and openly accessible resources for Mathematics, Physics and Computer Science, ranging from KS3 to undergraduate and Oxbridge-level study.

This page brings together selected educational resources, reading suggestions and open-access links that may support independent study in Mathematics, Physics, Computer Science and academic progression.

The resources below are grouped broadly by subject and level. They are not ranked and are not listed in order of preference. They appear to be free to access at the time of listing and are linked for convenience as general educational resources.

Inclusion on this page does not imply endorsement of any website, organisation, author, resource provider, content, views or teaching approach. I do not receive payment, sponsorship, commission or other benefit for listing any resource here.

All links point to external websites. I have not reproduced copyrighted textbook material, school-owned resources, internal assessment materials or pupil work on this page. Users should check the terms of use, copyright position and suitability of each resource for their own context.
